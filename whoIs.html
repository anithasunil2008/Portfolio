WHOIS


IBM Watson Personality Insights to analyze comments and reviews for people from their social media and consumer review posts. it will be of interest to someone who receives a comment or review to know more about the individual who posted the message. App will allow a user to run a personality analysis of a user to better understand the major traits of their personality









Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, Javascript,Firebase,Reddit API, Watson API, Materialize, Bootstrap, jQuery, Chart.js
